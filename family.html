In addition to my family, I am fortunate to have a tight-knit group of friends known as the “Velle Boys.” We are a group of seven, and we share a bond that’s built on fun, support, and a sense of adventure. We always have each other’s backs, whether it's tackling challenges, making memories, or just having a good time
